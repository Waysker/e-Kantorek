Visual Direction

ORAGH first style pass

Two grounded directions built from the AGH palette as a baseline, but adapted for a warmer orchestra community product. Both keep AGH green at the core and use red only as a rare accent.

Direction 01

AGH Formal

Closest to the university baseline. Crisp, restrained, and more institutional. Good if ORAGH should feel clearly tied to AGH at first glance.

Primary AGH green #00693C
Ink AGH black #1E1E1E
Accent AGH red, sparingly #A71930
Canvas Cool paper #F3F5F2
Surface Quiet white #FBFCFA
